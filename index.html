Discovering the Hidden Gems of the Amalfi Coast

By Sarah Chen | October 26, 2025


The Amalfi Coast is a legendary destination, but beyond the crowded streets of Positano and Amalfi lie villages frozen in time. This is a journey for those who seek tranquility, authentic Italian cuisine, and cliffside views that will genuinely take your breath away. We spent ten days exploring the quieter side of Campania, and here is what we discovered.


The Flavors of Cetara: Anchovies and Tradition

While many tourists focus on Capri, the tiny fishing village of Cetara offers a culinary experience unmatched anywhere else on the coast. Famous for its ancient tradition of curing anchovies, Cetara provides a taste of Italy that is both salty and soulfully authentic. Be sure to try the Colatura di Alici.


"Travel is the only thing you buy that makes you richer."


Hiking the Path of the Gods (Sentiero degli Dei)

If you're looking for adventure, the Path of the Gods is a must. This challenging but rewarding trail links Bomerano to Nocelle, offering unparalleled views high above the Mediterranean. Remember to bring plenty of water and wear sturdy shoes.


Things to Pack

  • Sturdy Hiking Boots
  • Sunscreen and Hat
  • Rechargeable Fan (for summer)
  • Universal Adapter
  • A good phrasebook

Whether you prefer relaxing on a secluded beach or scaling ancient cliffs, the Amalfi Coast holds a treasure trove of experiences. By stepping just slightly off the main tourist path, you can find a depth of culture and beauty that makes the journey truly unforgettable. Buon viaggio!
